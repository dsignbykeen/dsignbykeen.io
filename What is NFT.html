WHAT IS NFT?

NFT is a non-fungible token digital asset with a unique identification code and data which is secured on decentralized blockhain system. NFT can only have single owner at a time, the unique identification gives owner the ability to transfer or trade NFT assets recorded on the Ethereum Blockchain,a distributed ledger.

NFT can represent digital or non digital asset allowing owners to own a digital copy of the original asset through collectibles of arts,music,photos,audios,and other unique physical items.

According to Ethereum.org NFTs are tokens that we can use to represent ownership of unique items. They let us tokenise things like art, collectibles, even real estate.
